Project: Future Displays
Year: 2023
The wooden displays were featured at the 'Future' station during the Radical Times exhibition hosted by @viskom_linz. Visitors were encouraged to participate in discussions and contribute their visions for the future.
The displays showcased silkscreen printing on wood. The process involved responding to a question on a scale, and T-shirts were created for the exhibition team.
